Student attendance management system deals with the maintenance of the student’s attendance details.

“SMART Attendance Management System” is software developed for maintaining the attendance of the
student in the college. This system will also help in evaluating attendance eligibility criteria of a student. Report of the student’s attendance on monthly basis is generated.

This application mainly contains of 3 modules for a perfect and a proper functioning.
The modules are as follows:
1.Registration Form
2.Attendence Status
3.About

Registration Form :

The "Registration Form" module consists of a basic form to store the data or details of a student in the database.
the student data is entered or inserted into the database only at his/her admission in the college.

Attendence Status :

The "Attendence Status" module intern consists of two more module as follows:
(i). Edit Attendence
(ii). View Attendence

Edit Attendence :

The " Edit Attendence " module is one used for the entry of student attendence for a particular month in an academic year .
The faculty has to enter the details such as Branch , Year / Semester , Shift , No . of working days , No. of pesent days and Pin no. of the student .

View Attendence :

The "View Attendence" module provides a table that contains attendence of students of a class. This module just views and denials editing of attendence.In this module the user just need to enter or select the details such as smeester and month they want to view.The application then provides the list of all the students belonging to that year and their attendence for that specified month.
